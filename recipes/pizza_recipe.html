Pizza Recipe

This is a very delicious dish, it is very good. You can eat it a lot and get very full. It is good.

Ingredients

  • Pizza crust
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Directions

  1. Spread tomato sauce on crust
  2. Sprinkle cheese on sauce
  3. Place pepperoni around top
  4. Bake at 350 for 15 minutes
  5. Remove from oven and let cool
